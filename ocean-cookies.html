Ocean Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Fish Merge Game ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized content.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our site. Cookies can be either "session cookies" (temporary) or "persistent cookies" (stored for longer periods).

In addition to cookies, we may use other similar technologies such as:

  • Web Beacons: Small graphic images that help us track website usage
  • Local Storage: Browser storage that persists data locally
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that lasts only for the current session
  • Fingerprinting: Technology that identifies devices based on their characteristics

Types of Cookies We Use

We use several categories of cookies for different purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Authentication Cookies: Remember your login status and session information
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and ensure secure connections
  • Load Balancing Cookies: Distribute website traffic across servers
  • Functionality Cookies: Enable core website features and navigation

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Analytics Cookies: Track page views, time on site, and user behavior
  • Error Tracking: Monitor and report website errors and performance issues
  • Load Time Monitoring: Measure page load speeds and optimize performance
  • User Journey Tracking: Analyze how users navigate through our site

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language, region, and display preferences
  • Personalization Cookies: Customize content based on your interests and behavior
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable social media sharing and integration features
  • Third-Party Service Cookies: Support external services like maps and chat widgets

Targeting and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and content:

  • Retargeting Cookies: Show you relevant ads based on your previous visits
  • Affiliate Cookies: Track referrals from partner websites
  • Conversion Tracking: Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Audience Segmentation: Group users for targeted content delivery

Specific Cookies We Use

Here are the specific cookies we use on our website:

Our Own Cookies

  • fishmerge_session: Maintains your session while browsing (expires when browser closes)
  • user_preferences: Stores your display and language preferences (expires in 1 year)
  • consent_status: Remembers your cookie consent choices (expires in 1 year)
  • last_visit: Tracks when you last visited our site (expires in 30 days)

Third-Party Cookies

  • Google Analytics: _ga, _gid, _gat cookies for website analytics
  • Google Ads: _gcl_au for conversion tracking
  • Facebook Pixel: _fbp for advertising and analytics
  • Hotjar: _hjid for user behavior analysis

Cookie Duration

Cookies have different lifespans depending on their purpose:

  • Session Cookies: Automatically deleted when you close your browser
  • Temporary Cookies: Last for a few hours or days
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for months or years
  • Third-Party Cookies: Duration controlled by the third-party service

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies and similar technologies:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Cookie Consent Tool

We provide a cookie consent tool that allows you to:

  • Accept or decline different categories of cookies
  • Modify your preferences at any time
  • Withdraw consent for specific cookie types
  • View detailed information about each cookie category

Third-Party Opt-Outs

You can opt out of third-party cookies through these services:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Preferences
  • Digital Advertising Alliance: Your Ad Choices
  • Network Advertising Initiative: NAI Opt-Out

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Website may not function properly if disabled
  • Performance Cookies: We won't be able to optimize site performance
  • Functional Cookies: Personalization features may not work
  • Targeting Cookies: Ads may be less relevant to your interests

Mobile and App Cookies

When you use our services on mobile devices or through mobile apps, we may use similar technologies such as:

  • Mobile Device IDs: Unique identifiers for mobile devices
  • App Analytics: Tracking app usage and performance
  • Push Notification Tokens: For sending notifications
  • Location Data: With your consent for location-based features
